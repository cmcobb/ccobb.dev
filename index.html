Things I Do

Photography & Videography

My passion may be in creativity, but my goal is to share what's important. Sometimes, what is important is a story, or maybe even just a moment. Other times it could even be an idea! But my goal is to communicate to others why it's worth knowing about, and photography and videography are the perfect medium.

Social Media & Marketing

If the goal is to share what's important, you've got to know how to get it out there! I've been lucky to work with a variety of organizations, from marketing agencies, to non-profits, to ministries! Each context has a different target audience, and a different way to reach that audience.

And Even
Logistics

Sometimes the things we want to share haven't happened yet! Maybe it's planning what an event will look like, making the schedule, or even finding the right people to get things on a roll! If you need to make sure your conference is photographed, maybe there's a stage prop you need, or something else, I can help!

May

The Distro Application

A (not so) little known fact is that my Bachelor's is in Computer Science! For my senior project, I lead a group of three other students in building a web app called 'Distro'. Distro's goal was to be a warehouse management database website, inspired by a need for inventory management at that coffee shop I worked at that had opened it's first new location! While the application had approximately 80% completion by the time of submission, we are still proud of what was accomplished! It featured separate user panels for four different types of users (site admins, business owners, warehouse managers, and store managers), account creation (with a verification process to link accounts to businesses), and a fair bit more! A link to our final report can be found here, and our presentation here!
