How I Bring Ideas to Life

Photography & Videography

My passion lies in creativity, but my goal is to share what truly matters. Whether it’s a compelling story, a fleeting moment, or a bold idea—I aim to communicate why it’s worth knowing and remembering. Photography and videography provide the ideal medium to bring that message to life.

Social Media & Marketing

If your goal is to share what matters, you also need to know how to share it. I’ve been fortunate to work across a variety of organizations, from marketing agencies to non-profits to ministries, helping each develop strategy to reach and engage their own unique audience through digital and print media.

And
More!

Creativity often goes hand in hand with versatility. Beyond media and marketing, I also have experience in design, programming, and database management. Whether it’s designing promotional materials, developing digital tools, or organizing the systems behind the scenes, I enjoy bringing ideas to life from both the creative and technical sides.

A-State BCM - Website Update

Toward the end of the Spring 2024 semester, we realized that it was probably time for an update on the website! May

The Distro Application

A (not so) little known fact is that my Bachelor's is in Computer Science! For my senior project, I lead a group of three other students in building a web app called 'Distro'. Distro's goal was to be a warehouse management database website, inspired by a need for inventory management at that coffee shop I worked at that had opened it's first new location! While the application had approximately 80% completion by the time of submission, we are still proud of what was accomplished! It featured separate user panels for four different types of users (site admins, business owners, warehouse managers, and store managers), account creation (with a verification process to link accounts to businesses), and a fair bit more! A link to our final report can be found here, and our presentation here!
